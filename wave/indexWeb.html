Led by the legendary high school football coach Quincy! - this class is designed to instill discipline, teamwork, and perseverance in the students both on and off the field. Music

Our Music program is the perfect opportunity for students to explore their musical talents and learn the ins and outs of music production, - including DJ skills and music theory, with the guidance of a professional teacher who has worked with popular producers and has their own label. Students will get hands-on experience creating their own music and building their confidence in expressing themselves creatively.

Join us today and see just how much you can achieve!

Join us today and unleash your creativity while having fun!

Join us today and discover your passion for coding and game design!
