Cooking

Introducing a cooking class like no other - led by an expert teacher with more than 15 years of experience in teaching cooking, and supported by enthusiastic assistants, this class is designed to engage middle school students in a fun and educational cooking experience.

Through this program, students will develop valuable life skills such as meal planning, nutrition, and food preparation. The classes will be interactive, with students working in groups to create delicious dishes while learning about the science of cooking.

Our expert teacher will guide students through the entire cooking process, providing step-by-step instructions and insights into the culinary arts. He has a passion for teaching cooking and enjoys working with young people, helping them discover the joys of creating delicious and healthy meals.

The program will cover a range of cuisines, including international dishes, and students will be encouraged to explore their creativity by adding their own unique twists to the recipes. They will learn about different flavor combinations, spices, and herbs, and how to use them to create delicious and healthy meals.

In addition to the practical skills learned in the kitchen, this program also emphasizes the importance of food safety and hygiene. Students will learn about the proper handling and storage of food, and how to prevent cross-contamination and other hazards.

By participating in this cooking class, students will gain a sense of accomplishment, confidence, and independence as they develop valuable life skills. They will leave the program with a newfound appreciation for food and cooking, and a desire to continue learning and exploring in the kitchen.

Join us for an exciting culinary adventure and learn to cook like a pro!

Sports

Led by the legendary high school football coach Quincy! - this class is designed to engage middle school students in a fun and educational cooking experience.

Coach Quincy has led his team to countless victories and championships. He is passionate about helping young athletes reach their full potential and develop the skills they need to succeed on and off the field.

In this class, students will have the opportunity to work directly with Coach Quincy and his team of experienced coaches, learning the fundamentals of football, as well as other sports such as basketball, soccer, and volleyball. They will receive personalized training and feedback to help them improve their skills and reach their athletic goals.

But this class is not just about sports - Coach Quincy also places a strong emphasis on teamwork, sportsmanship, and leadership. Students will learn valuable life skills such as communication, responsibility, and perseverance, all while having fun and building lasting friendships with their classmates.

So whether your child is an aspiring athlete or simply looking to improve their fitness and have some fun, this sports class with Coach Quincy is the perfect opportunity for them to learn from one of the best coaches in the business and take their skills to the next level!

Join us for an exceptional sports program and have fun with friends!
